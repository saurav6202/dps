### **1. Homepage (Attractive & Informative)**
- School name, logo, and tagline.
- A welcome message or a brief introduction.
- High-quality images of the school campus.

### **2. About Us**
- History of the school.
- Mission, vision, and values.
- Message from the principal or director.

### **3. Academics**
- List of classes and subjects offered.
- Curriculum details.
- Teaching methodology.

### **4. Admissions**
- Admission process and eligibility.
- Online inquiry/contact form.
- Downloadable admission forms (if applicable).

### **5. Faculty & Staff**
- Profiles of key staff members (Principal, teachers, etc.).
- Photos and short descriptions.

### **6. Student Life & Activities**
- Events, clubs, and extracurricular activities.
- Achievements and awards.

### **7. Gallery**
- Images of classrooms, events, and campus.
- Option for a slideshow.

### **8. Contact Us**
- School address and Google Maps integration.
- Phone number and email.
- Contact form for inquiries.

### **9. Notices & Announcements**
- A dynamic section for latest news and updates.
- Upcoming events and holidays.

### **10. Responsive Design**
- Ensure the website looks good on mobile, tablet, and desktop.




--------------- layout -----------

### **Basic Layout Idea (Wireframe Description)**

#### **1️⃣ Header (Top Navigation Bar)**
- Logo on the left.
- Navigation links: **Home | About | Academics | Admissions | Faculty | Gallery | Contact**
- A "Notices" button for important announcements.

#### **2️⃣ Hero Section (Main Banner)**
- A big **welcome message** with a background image of the school.
- A **"Learn More"** button linking to the "About Us" page.

#### **3️⃣ About Section**
- A short paragraph about the school.
- Principal’s message or an image of the school.

#### **4️⃣ Academics Section**
- A grid showing different **classes & subjects**.
- Brief description of the school’s education system.

#### **5️⃣ Admissions Section**
- Steps to apply for admission.
- A contact form for inquiries.

#### **6️⃣ Faculty Section**
- Photos of key teachers & staff with short descriptions.

#### **7️⃣ Gallery**
- A simple image slider showing school activities.

#### **8️⃣ Contact Section**
- Address, phone, email.
- Embedded **Google Maps** location.
- A **contact form** for visitors to send messages.

#### **9️⃣ Footer**
- Quick links to important pages.
- Copyright info & social media links (if any).
